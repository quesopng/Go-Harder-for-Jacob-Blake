Who is Jacob Blake?

Jacob Blake is a twenty-nine year old father of three who was shot seven times by Kenosha police on August 23, 2020.

2 / 3

What Happened to Jacob Blake?

Although there are some details still unknown, as Blake was walking to his car after breaking up a dispute between two women, Kenosha police officers grabbed him from the back and shot him seven times in the back while his children looked on.

3 / 3

Why Should I Care About Jacob Blake?

We should care about what happened because it demonstrates police brutality in the United States. Unfortunately, what happened to Jacob Blake happens to many other black people in the US. By bringing attention to this crime, we can push for a future where the injustices commited against Blake are not commited to anyone else.
